French omelette

Ingredients

  • 4 Eggs;
  • 2 Tablespoons of butter (or oil);
  • Chives, as needed;
  • Salt and pepper to taste.

Preparation

Beat the eggs with a wisk

Preheat the pan on medium heat, then add the butter and let it come to temp

Pour the eggs, stir for the first minute, sprinkle the chives and then let it cook until the desired doneness

Serve immediately, seasoning to taste
