Avocado Toast

Ingredients

  • 2 thick slices of bread;
  • 1 medium avocado
  • 2 Eggs;
  • 2 Tablespoon of butter (or oil);
  • Half a lemon
  • Salt, pepper and hot pepper flakes to taste.

Preparation

Remove the flesh of the avocado and mix it in a bowl with the lemon juice, salt, pepper and pepper flakes

Preheat the pan on medium heat, then add the butter and let it come to temp

Toast the bread in the butter, then add some more butter and let it heat too

Cook the eggs sunny side up or over easy

Assemble your toast: bread, avocado, then egg. Remember to season your eggs too!
